Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Thunder Chicken
  2. Mcdonald's
  3. Sunny Side
  4. Nam Dae Moon
  5. West Coast

Thunder Chicken | Chalan Kiya, Saipan

Thunder Chicken serves a plate of spicy chicken which in my opinion is better than KFC's version of spicy chicken. They also serve a variety of chicken as well as other side dishes. It is especially great when dining in with friends because they offer rooms to dine in that include karaoke. 

What I love:
  • The greatest spicy chicken
  • Nice karaoke room
  • Awesome side dishes
